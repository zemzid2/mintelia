About Us

Our
Story

Mintelia was built on a belief that the spaces you live and work in shape how you feel every day.

Who We Are

Craftsmanship at the
heart of everything we do

Mintelia began in 2013 as a small workshop in Karachi with a single purpose: to create furniture that is as beautiful as it is functional. What started as a team of four passionate craftsmen has grown into a full-service furniture and interior fit-out company trusted by hundreds of clients.

"We don't just make furniture — we create the backdrop for life's most important moments."

Every Mintelia piece begins with a conversation. We listen deeply to understand how you live, how you work, and what you value — then we design and build furniture that fits your world perfectly.

Our Mission
To craft furniture that stands the test of time

We are dedicated to creating furniture of exceptional quality — thoughtfully designed, carefully built, and made to last for generations. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design thinking.

Our Vision
To be the most trusted name in furniture

We envision a world where every home and workspace is filled with furniture that brings genuine joy. Our goal is to be the first name that comes to mind when people think of quality and beautiful design.
