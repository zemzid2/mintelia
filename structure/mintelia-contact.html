FAQs

Common questions

How long does a custom furniture order take?
+
Most custom pieces take 6–8 weeks from order confirmation to delivery. Larger fit-out projects may take 10–14 weeks. We always confirm a timeline before you commit.
Do you offer a free consultation?
+
Yes — all initial consultations are completely free. You can visit our showroom, schedule a home visit, or do a virtual call. No obligation at all.
Can I see a 3D preview before placing an order?
+
Absolutely. Our design team creates detailed 3D renders of your space before any furniture is built. You can request as many revisions as you need.
What areas do you deliver to?
+
We currently deliver and install across all of Karachi. For large projects, we also travel to Lahore and Islamabad. Contact us for out-of-city quotes.
What warranty do you offer?
+
All Mintelia furniture comes with a minimum 1-year warranty. Commercial projects include a 2-year warranty. We also offer extended maintenance packages.
