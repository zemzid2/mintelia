About Mintelia

Where craft meets design

Mintelia was founded with a single belief: great furniture should be both beautiful and functional. Every piece we create is the result of careful design, quality materials, and skilled craftsmanship.

We work closely with our clients to understand their spaces and lifestyle — then deliver furniture that truly fits.
